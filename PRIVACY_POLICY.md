# Privacy Policy for Insomnia Butler

**Effective Date:** January 29, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Insomnia Butler ("we," "our," "us," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("Application").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Application.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

- **Account Information:** Email address, name, and profile information provided during registration through Google Sign-In
- **Journal Entries:** Personal journal content, mood tracking data, timestamps, and tags you create
- **Health & Wellness Data:** Sleep patterns, activity data, and health metrics you choose to share
- **App Usage:** Application logs, crashes, and error reports
- **Communications:** Messages you send to our support team

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Device Information:** Device type, OS version, app version, device ID
- **Usage Analytics:** Features used, pages visited, time spent, interaction patterns
- **Location Data:** Approximate location (if you grant permission)
- **Performance Data:** App performance metrics and crash logs

### 2.3 Third-Party Data

- **Google Sign-In:** Email, name, profile picture (with your consent)
- **Health Apps:** Integration with health tracking apps (only with explicit permission)

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

- **Service Delivery:** Creating and maintaining your account, providing core app features
- **Improvement:** Analyzing usage patterns to improve app performance and user experience
- **Security:** Detecting and preventing fraud, abuse, and security issues
- **Communication:** Sending you updates, support messages, and service notifications
- **Analytics:** Understanding user behavior and app performance
- **Legal Compliance:** Meeting legal and regulatory obligations

---

## 4. Data Storage & Security

### 4.1 Data Storage
- Your data is securely stored on our Serverpod backend servers
- Encrypted transmission using HTTPS/TLS protocols
- Regular security audits and updates

### 4.2 Security Measures
- End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
- Secure authentication via Google Sign-In
- Password hashing using industry-standard algorithms
- Regular backups and disaster recovery procedures
- Restricted access to user data

### 4.3 Data Retention
- Account data is retained as long as your account is active
- Journal entries and personal data are retained until you delete them
- We delete inactive accounts upon user request or after 2 years of inactivity
